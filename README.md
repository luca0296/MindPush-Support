MindPush — Digital Discipline
Official Support & Documentation

YOU ARE NOT DISCIPLINED. NOT YET.

The average person spends over 1,000 hours a year staring at their phone. MindPush is not a screen time tracker. It is a discipline enforcement system built for people who are done lying to themselves. Every second of access to your chosen apps must be earned through real physical output or deep mental work.

No output, no access. Simple. Brutal. Effective.

HOW IT WORKS
MindPush operates on a single, unbreakable rule: Earn before you consume.

Earn: Complete push-ups or squats using the built-in AI camera tracker, or run a timed deep focus session.

Convert: Every rep and every minute of focus converts directly into screen time balance.

Lock: When your balance hits zero, your blocked apps lock automatically via Apple's Screen Time API. There is no bypass.

THE OVERTIME DEBT SYSTEM

If you ignore the zero-balance limit and remain in a blocked app, the Overtime system activates:

Phase 1 (30–90s): 1:1 debt. Every second used costs a second earned.

Phase 2 (90–210s): 2× debt. The meter doubles.

Phase 3 (210–330s): 5× debt.

Lockdown (330s+): Apps are blocked automatically. −15 Integrity points and 15 minutes of flat debt are applied.

ENFORCEMENT MODULES
PHYSICAL MODULE — EARN WITH YOUR BODY

Using the front camera and on-device AI pose detection (Apple Vision Framework):

Real-time Tracking: Supports push-ups and squats.

Form Validation: Full range-of-motion required; partial reps are rejected.

Hard Mode (Elite): Enforces a 3-second isometric hold at the bottom of every rep for 2× time yield.

Integrity Check: Leaving the app mid-session forfeits all uncounted reps.

MENTAL MODULE — EARN WITH YOUR MIND

The Deep Work Protocol converts focused work into screen time:

Standard Protocol: Auto-pauses if you leave the app.

Face-Down Enforcement (Elite): Leaving the app or lifting the phone fails the session immediately.

Custom Intervals: Configurable Pomodoro-style work blocks and rounds.

THE INTEGRITY ENGINE
Your Integrity Score (0–100) measures your actual discipline. Every correct decision earns points; every failure costs them. At zero, the system restructures to be 15% harder automatically.

INTEGRITY TIERS:

95–100 · IRON — Unbreakable resolve. Bonus Access unlocked.

80–94 · DISCIPLINED — Consistent. Master of intent. Bonus Access unlocked.

50–79 · WAVERING — Cracks in the foundation. Potential without presence.

20–49 · WEAK — Controlled by impulse. Hardening-only changes allowed.

0–19 · COWARD — Total surrender. Full lockdown.

ELITE PROTOCOLS
Strict Mode: All Integrity penalties are doubled.

Safe Zones: Define GPS coordinates (Home, Gym, Office) where blocking suspends automatically via on-device geofencing.

Mood Check-In: Calibrates notification urgency and friction based on your psychological state.

PRIVACY & SECURITY
MindPush is Local-First.

No Accounts: No servers, no cloud sync, no data collection.

Camera Privacy: Frames are processed in RAM and never saved, recorded, or transmitted.

Location Privacy: Geofencing is handled entirely by iOS; your coordinates never leave the device.

Data Control: A "Hard Reset" option in settings wipes all local SwiftData permanently.
