APP NAME
MindPush Discipline


You are not disciplined. Not yet.

The average person spends over 1,000 hours a year staring at their phone. MindPush is not a screen time tracker. It is a discipline enforcement system — built for people who are done lying to themselves.

Every second of access to your chosen apps must be earned through real physical output or deep mental work. No output, no access. Simple. Brutal. Effective.

HOW IT WORKS
MindPush operates on a single, unbreakable rule: earn before you consume.

Complete push-ups or squats using the built-in AI camera tracker, or run a timed deep focus session. Every rep and every minute of focus converts directly into screen time balance. When your balance hits zero, your blocked apps lock — automatically, with no bypass.

If you ignore the limit and stay on your phone, the Overtime system activates. Debt accumulates across three escalating penalty phases. Miss the exit window and the app auto-blocks with maximum debt applied.

PHYSICAL MODULE — EARN WITH YOUR BODY
The front camera tracks your movement in real time using on-device AI pose detection. No data ever leaves your phone.
• Push-ups and squats both supported.
• Skeleton overlay for instant form feedback.
• Full range-of-motion validation: partial reps are rejected automatically.
• Hard Mode (Elite): enforces a 3-second isometric hold at the bottom of every rep, yielding 2× screen time.
• Voice rep counting keeps you in position.
• Leaving the app mid-session loses all uncounted reps.

MENTAL MODULE — EARN WITH YOUR MIND
The Deep Work Protocol converts focused work into screen time at a ratio you define.
• Standard Protocol: auto-pauses if you leave the app.
• Face-Down Enforcement (Elite): leaving the app or lifting the phone beyond a 5-second grace window immediately fails the session.
• Custom Pomodoro-style intervals with configurable work blocks, break durations, and rounds (Elite).
• Up to 10 saved routines (Elite).
• 1.5× time bonus for completing face-down sessions.

THE INTEGRITY ENGINE
Your Integrity Score (0–100) is the system's measure of your actual discipline — not your intention. Every correct decision earns points. Every cowardly one costs them.

At zero, the system does not just block you — it restructures everything to be harder. 
You built the hole. You climb out of it one decision at a time.

INTEGRITY TIERS:
• 95–100 · IRON — Total confidence. Bonus Access unlocked.
• 80–94 · DISCIPLINED — Consistent. Reliable. Bonus Access unlocked.
• 50–79 · WAVERING — Cracks in the foundation. Full access but watch the trend.
• 20–49 · WEAK — Hardening-only changes allowed. You don't get to make it easier.
• 0–19 · COWARD — Full lockdown. Earn your way back.

OVERTIME — THE DEBT SYSTEM
When your balance hits zero, you have 30 seconds of free grace. Walk away clean. If you don't:
• Phase 1 (30–90s): 1:1 debt. Every second on the app costs a second.
• Phase 2 (90–210s): 2× debt. The meter is doubling.
• Phase 3 (210–330s): 5× debt. Auto-block approaching.
• Lockdown (330s+): Apps blocked automatically. Debt applied.

Escalating push notifications fire throughout. The system does not whisper.

ENFORCEMENT PROTOCOLS (Elite)
• Strict Mode: All Integrity penalties doubled. For people who need to feel the full weight of their decisions.
• Active Hours: Define a custom enforcement window. Apps unlock naturally outside it.
• Safe Zones: Define GPS coordinates (home, gym, office) where blocking suspends automatically. Powered by on-device geofencing. Never transmitted.
• Session Chunks: Sessions over 25 minutes are broken into enforced 25-minute blocks, each followed by a mandatory 5-minute break.
• Bonus Access: Unlocks 15 minutes of unrestricted access.

MOOD CHECK-IN (Elite)
Log your psychological state once per day. The system personalises notifications to your current emotional parameter — working with your momentum when it's high, maximising friction when it's fragile. 

DAILY DISCIPLINE METRICS
• 7-day output history for physical and mental sessions (Elite).
• Full session history with earned time per session (Elite).
• Integrity event log: every delta, every reason, permanently recorded.

NOTIFICATIONS DESIGNED TO INTERRUPT YOU
MindPush does not send gentle reminders. It sends real-time warnings during overtime that escalate in urgency and interrupt level. Motivational notifications fire throughout the day, calibrated to your Integrity tier and mood state. At zero Integrity, all daily notifications switch to critical alerts.

• Location data: stored in your device's private container only. Never sent anywhere.

• Hard Reset wipes everything permanently at any time.
