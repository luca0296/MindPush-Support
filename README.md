APP NAME: MindPush Discipline

YOU ARE NOT DISCIPLINED. NOT YET.

The average person spends over 1,000 hours a year staring at their phone. MindPush is not a screen time tracker. It is a discipline enforcement system — built for people who are done lying to themselves.

Every second of access to your chosen apps must be earned through real physical output or deep mental work. No output, no access. Simple. Brutal. Effective.

HOW IT WORKS

MindPush operates on a single, unbreakable rule: earn before you consume.

Complete push-ups or squats using the built-in AI camera tracker, or run a timed deep focus session. Every rep and every minute of focus converts directly into screen time balance.

When your balance hits zero, your blocked apps lock — automatically, with no bypass.

If you ignore the limit and stay on your phone, the Overtime system activates. Debt accumulates across three escalating penalty phases. Miss the exit window and the app auto-blocks with maximum debt applied.

PHYSICAL MODULE — EARN WITH YOUR BODY

The front camera tracks your movement in real time using on-device AI pose detection. No data ever leaves your phone.

Push-ups and squats both supported.

Skeleton overlay for instant form feedback.

Full range-of-motion validation: partial reps are rejected automatically.

Hard Mode (Elite): enforces a 3-second isometric hold at the bottom of every rep, yielding 2x screen time.

Voice rep counting keeps you in position.

Leaving the app mid-session loses all uncounted reps.

MENTAL MODULE — EARN WITH YOUR MIND

The Deep Work Protocol converts focused work into screen time at a ratio you define.

Standard Protocol: auto-pauses if you leave the app.

Face-Down Enforcement (Elite): leaving the app or lifting the phone beyond a 5-second grace window immediately fails the session.

Custom Pomodoro-style intervals with configurable blocks (Elite).

Up to 10 saved routines (Elite).

1.5x time bonus for completing face-down sessions.

THE INTEGRITY ENGINE

Your Integrity Score (0 to 100) is the system's measure of your actual discipline — not your intention. Every correct decision earns points. Every cowardly one costs them.

At zero, the system does not just block you — it restructures everything to be harder. You built the hole. You climb out of it one decision at a time.

OVERTIME — THE DEBT SYSTEM

When your balance hits zero, you have 30 seconds of free grace. Walk away clean. If you don't:

Phase 1 (30 to 90s): 1:1 debt. Every second on the app costs a second.

Phase 2 (90 to 210s): 2x debt. The meter is doubling.

Phase 3 (210 to 330s): 5x debt. Auto-block approaching.

Lockdown (330s+): Apps blocked automatically. Debt applied.

Escalating push notifications fire throughout. The system does not whisper.
