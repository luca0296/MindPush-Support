MindPush — Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2026

Developer: Luca Pizza

App: MindPush Discipline

Platform: Apple iOS

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how MindPush ("the App", "we", "our") handles information when you use the MindPush application on your Apple iOS device. MindPush is built on a strict Local-First architecture. All data generated by your use of the App is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to any external server, third-party service, or the developer.

By using MindPush, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

2. Data We Process and Why

2.1 Camera and Body Motion Data (Physical Module)

What: When you use the Physical Module (push-up and squat tracking), MindPush accesses your device's front-facing camera to perform real-time body-pose detection.

How it is processed: All image processing is executed entirely on your device using Apple's Vision framework and the VNDetectHumanBodyPoseRequest API. The App identifies skeletal joint positions in real time to count repetitions and validate form.

What we do NOT do:

No video or image frames are ever recorded, saved to disk, or stored in any form.

No camera data is transmitted over the network, to the developer, or to any third party.

No biometric identifiers are derived or retained after the session ends.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing is necessary to perform the core service the user explicitly requests — physical session tracking.

2.2 Precise Location Data (Safe Zones Feature — Optional)

What: If you choose to enable the Safe Zones feature, MindPush requests "Always Allow" location access from iOS to implement on-device geofencing around coordinates you define.

How it is processed: Location coordinates are stored exclusively in the App's private App Group container on your device (group.com.lucapizza.mindpush). The App uses Apple's CoreLocation framework and CLCircularRegion APIs to monitor defined zones entirely on-device.

What we do NOT do:

No location data is ever transmitted to the developer or any external server.

No location history or movement logs are created.

Location is sampled using a single requestLocation() call (not continuous GPS polling) to determine your current zone status, then the GPS radio shuts down immediately.

Optional nature: This feature is entirely optional. The App functions fully without location access. If you deny location permission, Safe Zones are disabled automatically and all other features remain unaffected.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)): Processing is based on your explicit consent, granted by enabling the Safe Zones feature and authorising "Always Allow" location access within iOS Settings.

Withdrawal of consent: You may revoke location permission at any time via iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → MindPush. Revoking permission disables Safe Zones and removes all geofence monitoring.

2.3 Session and Performance Data (Discipline Metrics)

What: The App records your session history, including:

Push-up and squat session data (rep count, duration, time earned, exercise type, hard mode flag)

Deep Work session data (duration in seconds, date)

Integrity Score and all Integrity delta events (reason text, point value, timestamp)

App settings and configuration (rep targets, time ratios, free minute allowance, scheduling preferences)

Mood check-in state (current mood, daily change count, feature enabled flag)

How it is processed: All data is stored locally using Apple's SwiftData framework, in a database file located within the App's private App Group container on your device. This container is inaccessible to other apps and is not backed up to iCloud (CloudKit sync is explicitly disabled).

What we do NOT do: None of this data is transmitted, shared, or accessible to the developer or any third party.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing is necessary to deliver the core discipline-tracking functionality of the App.

2.4 Purchase and Subscription Data (In-App Purchases)

What: MindPush offers optional Elite tier access via in-app purchase (yearly subscription and lifetime one-time payment).

How it is processed: All purchase transactions are handled exclusively by Apple via the StoreKit framework. MindPush only receives a cryptographically verified transaction receipt from Apple, which is processed locally on-device to confirm Elite status. The App stores a boolean flag locally (isPremiumUser) indicating whether Elite access is active.

What we do NOT do: The App does not collect, store, or transmit your payment information, Apple ID, or any financial data. We have no access to billing details.

Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing is necessary to verify and activate the paid features you have purchased.

2.5 Notifications

What: If you grant notification permission, MindPush schedules local push notifications entirely on-device for:

Daily motivational messages

Overtime escalation alerts during active sessions

Mood check-in reminders

Session milestone notifications

Hard Reset countdown warnings

How it is processed: All notifications are created and scheduled locally using Apple's UserNotifications framework. No notification content is transmitted to any server or external push notification service (no APNs server-side push is used).

What we do NOT do: No notification interaction data is collected or reported.

3. Data We Do NOT Collect

MindPush does not collect, process, or transmit any of the following:

Names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any contact information

Device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, or similar)

Crash reports or analytics telemetry

Advertising data or cross-app tracking signals

Health & Fitness data via Apple HealthKit

Photos or media from your photo library

Microphone audio (voice synthesis for rep counting uses AVSpeechSynthesizer output only — no audio input is captured)

Contacts, Calendar, or any other system data

4. Third-Party SDKs and Services

MindPush does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or crash-reporting SDKs. The App relies exclusively on Apple's native frameworks:

Vision — on-device body pose detection

CoreLocation — on-device geofencing

FamilyControls / ManagedSettings / DeviceActivity — on-device app blocking via Apple Screen Time API

SwiftData — local persistent storage

StoreKit — purchase verification

UserNotifications — local notifications

ActivityKit — Live Activity display (local only)

BackgroundTasks — local background enforcement tasks

AVFoundation / CoreMotion — camera and motion sensor access

No data passes through any third-party infrastructure.

5. Apple Platform Privacy

MindPush uses Apple's Family Controls entitlement and Screen Time API to implement app blocking. This system is managed by Apple's operating system and operates without the developer having visibility into which specific apps are blocked. App token selection data is stored locally on your device.
MindPush does not participate in Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework because it performs no cross-app or cross-site tracking.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as the App is installed.
To delete all data permanently, use the Hard Reset feature located in the App's Configuration tab → System → Initiate Hard Reset. This irreversibly erases all session history, Integrity events, settings, and metrics from your device.
Uninstalling the App from your device will also remove all associated data stored in the App's private container.

7. Children's Privacy

MindPush is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Economic Area). The App does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has used the App and provided personal information, please contact us immediately using the details in Section 11.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR (EEA and UK Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) or the UK GDPR:

Right of Access: You have the right to know what personal data we process. Given MindPush's local-first architecture, all data is visible directly within the App's interface.

Right to Rectification: Session and settings data can be modified directly within the App.

Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can permanently delete all data at any time using the Hard Reset feature, or by uninstalling the App.

Right to Restriction of Processing: You can disable specific data-collection features (e.g., Safe Zones, Mood Check-in) within the App's settings.

Right to Data Portability: Data is stored in Apple's SwiftData format on-device. Export functionality is not currently offered, as all data remains exclusively yours and on your device.

Right to Object: You may object to any data processing by disabling the relevant feature or using Hard Reset.

Right to Withdraw Consent: For features based on consent (Safe Zones / location), consent may be withdrawn at any time via iOS Settings.

Because all data is stored locally on your device and is never accessible to the developer, exercising these rights is primarily accomplished through the App's own interface and iOS system settings.

9. Your Rights Under CCPA (California Users)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion of personal information, and to opt out of the sale of personal information. MindPush does not sell, share, or disclose personal information to any third party. All data remains on your device. You may delete all data at any time via the Hard Reset feature.
